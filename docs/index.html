Layers
A full editing suite

Everything you need to compose an image.

Stack layers, paint freely, grade colour, add type, and cut stencils — all with undo/redo and no round-trip to the cloud.

Layers & blend modes

Stack, reorder, link and duplicate layers, each with opacity and its own blend mode.

Brush, eraser & liquify

Paint, erase, smudge and liquify with pressure-friendly strokes and a live preview.

Curves & colour

Tone curves, colour balance, HSL, brightness and contrast — tune the whole image or one layer.

Text layers

Add editable type with Google Fonts, kerning, outline and shadow — rasterise when you're ready.

Stencil & poster

Turn art into cut-ready stencils or tile it into a multi-page poster PDF for large prints.

AI background removal

Isolate the subject in a tap with on-device segmentation — no upload, no waiting.

LUTs & extensions

Apply installable colour LUTs and grade with one tap for a consistent, repeatable look.

Export & hand-off

Save a composite, or share it straight to another app — including GraffitiXR for AR.

Non-destructive

Every adjustment is a layer you can revisit, with full undo/redo history throughout.
